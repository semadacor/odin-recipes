Gloton's Falafel

Ingredients

  • Falafel
  • Two slices of bread
  • Yellow chili pepper
  • Cheese
  • Mushrooms
  • Corn

Steps

  1. Prepare the falafel

  2. Prepare the yellow chili pepper, the cheese, the mushrooms, and the corn.

  3. Between the two slices of bread put every ingredient you have prepared.

  4. Add your favorite sauces.

  5. Eat that delicious beast.
